How do I start earning with EARNZY?

Simply sign up with your Google account or mobile number, complete the verification, and you'll receive ₹2 as a signup bonus. Then browse available tasks and start earning!

How do I withdraw my earnings?

You can withdraw your earnings once you reach the minimum balance of ₹25. Go to the Withdraw section, choose your preferred method (UPI, Paytm, or bank transfer), and request withdrawal. Free users get payments within 24-48 hours, while premium users get instant withdrawals.

Is EARNZY completely free to use?

Yes, the basic version of EARNZY is completely free. You can earn money without any investment. We also offer premium subscription plans that give you higher rewards and faster withdrawals, but they're optional.

How are tasks verified?

After completing a task, you submit proof (screenshot or text) through our system. Our admin team reviews each submission manually to ensure task completion meets requirements. This process usually takes 1-6 hours.

Can I use EARNZY on multiple devices?

For security reasons, each account is limited to one primary device. Our system tracks devices to prevent fraud. If you need to change devices, contact our support team for assistance.
